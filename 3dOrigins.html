Interactive Timeline

  • 1984 - Invention of the 3d printer

    • Charles (Chuck) Hull, invents the first type of 3d printing - Stereolithography

  • 1986 - The term Stereolithography is created

    • Charles Hull, created the term Stereolithography when he patented his new machine.

  • 1992 - First 3d printer is built

    • The first Stereolithographic apparatus (SLA) is built by the company 3d systems.

  • 1998 - First 3d printer (SLA) is sold

    • The first Stereolithographic apparatus (SLA) is sold.

  • 1999 - First organ is printed

    • The first 3d organ, a bladder is printed with the patients own cells so the body won't reject it.

  • 2000 - Working 3d printed organs

    • Scientist engineer a 3d printed kidney that is able to filter blood

  • 2006 - Printing in multiple materials

    • Objet (A 3d systems and materials company) creates a 3d printer that can print in multiple materials such as metal, plastic, and rubber.

  • 2008 - First usable prosthetic leg is printed

    • The first usable prosthetic leg is printed and requires no assembly.

  • 2009 - 3d printed blood vessels

    • Organavo, creates the first 3d printed blood vessels.

  • 2011 - Printing gold and silver

    • Printing service i.materialize begins to offer 14 carot gold and sterling silver to be printed
